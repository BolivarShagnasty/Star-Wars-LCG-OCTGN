=====Message of the Day======

The Legends of the Force fan-made cycle has been added to the game unscripted. These cards are
currently unofficical, but are being playtested by the Star Wars LCG Council (the
volunteer player committee providing continuing support for the game). If using these
cards please make note in your room name.

Please submit playtesting feedback here:
http://www.cardgamedb.com/forums/index.php?/topic/40027-thoughts-about-the-legends-cycle-report-playtesting-feedback-here/

Come join us on the SW:LCG Discord Server. You can text chat with other players as well as
use the voice channels to interact with your OCTGN opponents.
https://discordapp.com/invite/0shXbwedwlOcu64k

To report any issues with current scripting, please visit:
https://github.com/BolivarShagnasty/Star-Wars-LCG-OCTGN/issues
